Seasonal Prakriti

How seasons shift your dosha

Ayurveda teaches that each season aggravates a specific dosha. Your Prakriti is fixed — but your balance shifts with the seasons.

Your Seasonal Dosha Balance

How each season amplifies or calms each dosha

Ayurvedic wisdom: Knowing your season's dominant dosha helps you counter-balance — e.g. a Pitta person in summer (Pitta season) should be especially careful with heat, spice, and overwork. A Kapha person in spring should exercise more and eat lighter to counter Kapha accumulation.

About This Project

Research & Team

An undergraduate research project exploring AI applications in traditional Ayurvedic medicine.

🎯 Research Objective

To build an AI-powered Prakriti classification system and analyse how synthetic (AI-generated) survey data compares to real human responses — identifying biases, gaps, and limitations of using LLMs to augment health survey datasets.

🛠️ Tech Stack

Python Flask scikit-learn Random Forest SQLite HTML/CSS/JS Chart.js Claude API GitHub Pages

👩‍💻 Project By

Ankita S.
AI in Ayurveda Research Project
GitHub →

📋 Limitations

• Small human sample (n=61) limits generalisability
• AI data from a single LLM — may not represent all synthetic approaches
• Self-reported Prakriti may differ from clinical diagnosis
• Age range skewed young (avg 22.4 years)
Methodology

How this study was conducted

An 18-question Google Form covering physical traits, physiological patterns, psychological tendencies, and lifestyle preferences.

1

Survey Design

18-question Prakriti assessment built on classical Ayurvedic dosha markers across body type, skin, sleep, digestion, mind, stress, and taste.

2

Human Data Collection

61 real human responses via Google Forms. Participants aged 13–55, mix of genders.

3

AI Data Generation

128 synthetic responses generated using LLM-based simulation to augment the dataset across all three Prakriti types.

4

ML Classification

Random Forest classifier trained on all 18 features to predict Prakriti. Encoders handle categorical features; scaler normalises inputs.

5

Comparative Analysis

Statistical comparison of human vs AI distributions across all questions, demographic breakdowns, and discordance between Q16/Q18.

6

Web Application

Flask app with user login, prediction, Vikriti assessment, symptom checker, meal planner, PDF reports, and admin dashboard.
